# Service-Based SaaS w/ Inventory Playbook
## Phase 1 – Discovery
- Document revenue streams, subscription billing, and hardware fulfillment touchpoints.
- Align compliance (SOC 2, ASC 606) and asset tracking expectations.

## Phase 2 – Data Preparation
- Clean customer master, subscription plans, device catalog, and serialized asset registers.
- Import deferred revenue balances, RMA statuses, and partner inventories.

## Phase 3 – Configuration
- Enable subscription billing, milestone revenue recognition, and device lifecycle states.
- Configure project/service tickets tied to hardware shipments and warranty pools.

## Phase 4 – Integration & Automation
- Integrate CRM, subscription gateway, RMM/IoT telemetry, and support desk tools.
- Automate provisioning workflows, RMAs, and contract renewal alerts.

## Phase 5 – Pilot
- Pilot with one region’s install base and top service partners.
- Validate deferred revenue postings, swap logistics, and SLA escalations.

## Phase 6 – Rollout
- Migrate remaining customers in cohorts; sync support teams on unified dashboards.
- Provide knowledge base playbooks for finance, support, and field ops.

## Phase 7 – Post Go-Live
- Monitor ARR, churn, device utilization, and service margins; iterate automation rules and asset pool sizing.
