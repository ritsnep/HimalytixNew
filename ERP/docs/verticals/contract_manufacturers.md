# Contract Manufacturer Go-Live Playbook
## Phase 1 – Discovery
- Capture customer-specific BOM revisions, quality checkpoints, and compliance docs.
- Map resource capacities, work centers, and outsourcing partners.

## Phase 2 – Data Preparation
- Normalize multilevel BOMs, routings, and tooling libraries.
- Import supplier lead times, QC specs, and WIP snapshots.

## Phase 3 – Configuration
- Enable engineer-to-order templates, batch/serial control, yield tracking, and NCR workflows.
- Configure production calendars, labor/overhead absorption rules, and customer portals.

## Phase 4 – Integration & Automation
- Connect PLM/CAD for BOM sync, MES for shop-floor feedback, and EDI for customer forecasts.
- Schedule MRP runs, automatic purchase suggestions, and deviation alerts.

## Phase 5 – Pilot
- Run limited-scope pilot on two representative products.
- Validate cost rollups, subcontracting loops, and CoA postings.

## Phase 6 – Rollout
- Train planners, production supervisors, QA, and finance with role-based dashboards.
- Phase in remaining product families while monitoring bottlenecks.

## Phase 7 – Post Go-Live
- Review OEE, schedule adherence, scrap, and margin variance; refine routings, buffer sizes, and supplier scorecards.
